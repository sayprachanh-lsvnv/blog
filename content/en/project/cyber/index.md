## 🖥️ Course about the cybersecurity

For the past few days, I completed the course on the fundamentals of cybersecurity and I will share some of my experience about the course. Firstly, this course is about basic of cybersecurity which covers a lot of concept about well... __CYBERSECURITY!!__ and some stuff about networking, website and computer protection and etc, it maybe a bit much, but rest assured, this course is also completely for beginner who are interested in cybersecurity or want to know a bit about such field.

---

## 🛜 Network security

The first section of the course, focuses on how the internet works, which covered such as 

- Basic networks protocols - TCP/IP, HTTP, and others,
- Network personalization for how the settings affect security,
- The risk and protection of the Wireless Wi-Fi networks 

Additionally, it introduced some usages and concept of Tor browser and other anonymization methods. Personally, the Tor module was especially interesting — it really showed how online anonymity works in practice.

---

## 📱 PC/Phone protection

Next up, the second section, which covered about personal digital hygiene and the protection of personal device such as 
- Disk encryption
- The idea of strong passwords and password managers.
- The concept of Phishing 
- Viruses with real-world examples and protection methods.
- Messenger security

---

## 🔗 Cryptography in practice

The final part of the course, the most  interesting and challenging. In this section will covered the important parts such as 

- Introduction to cryptography (history, algorithms, why it's important)
- Blockchain (no hype, just clear example) 

As well as the topic about digital signatures and verify authenticity, Electronic payments and transaction security.

---

## 🏁 Overall

This is a really great course for introduction to the cybersecurity, since it covered a lot of concept about such field, plus give some ideas about protection and how some systems work in general. 

Unfortunately, This course doesn't provide any certificate of completion (sad...😞), but we received something more important... knowledges... (unless you need the certificate for something else...🤨). Anyway, this's it for now. Thank you for reading the post!
